How to setup Wanchain on a ledger nano s.

  1. get a ledger nano s.

    Ledger Store

  2. set up the ledger nano s.

    Nano S Getting Started

  3. While following those instructions, install the "Ethereum" Ledger nano app from the Ledger Manager chrome app.
  4. Open the "Ethereum" app on your ledger nano s.
  5. Install the WanWalletGui app.

    Wanchain Wallet

  6. Open the WanWalletGui app. The account with all zeroes as it's private key is the account on your ledger nano s.
